About Us

Meet Myra and Jairo, a dynamic husband-and-wife duo with a shared passion for house music.

Meet Myra and Jairo, a dynamic husband-and-wife duo who have been married for 25 years and spinning beats as DJs throughout Chicago for just as long. With a shared love for house music, they’ve built a reputation for creating unforgettable vibes at events of all kinds from high-energy public gatherings to intimate private parties. As passionate entertainers, Myra and Jairo bring their unique chemistry and decades of experience to every performance, keeping dance floors alive and audiences engaged. Beyond their DJing, they share their artistry with fans through exclusive merchandise, including stylish t-shirts and USBs filled with their signature mixes. Whether you’re looking for event entertainment or a fresh way to enjoy house music, Myra and Jairo are here to deliver the soundtrack to your unforgettable moments.
